Our History

Journey of Succor Technical Services: From Solo Start to Competitive Expansion.

Succor Technical Services was founded by Marian Ayo Fawemimo in December 2017, following her departure from a leading healthcare provider in New York on November 27, 2017. With a solid foundation in Computer Information Systems and years of extensive experience, Marian launched her technology venture. Initially steering the company on her own, she later expanded the team by bringing on board professionals with deep expertise in information technology. Since its inception, Succor Technical Services has evolved dramatically, now competing for government contracts and securing projects from diverse private sector enterprises.

Our upcoming objective involves global expansion, extending our services to businesses worldwide. Additionally, Succor holds a strong commitment to supporting underprivileged communities. In the near future, we aspire to fund eligible students' school tuition through our scholarship program.
