Our History

From a Single Vision to a Growing Consulting Enterprise.

Succor Technical Services, LLC was founded in December 2017 by Marian Ayo Fawemimo, following her tenure at a leading healthcare organization in New York. With a strong background in Computer Information Systems and extensive experience in workforce management and technology, Marian established the company with a clear vision—to deliver impactful, high-quality solutions that address real business challenges.

What began as a solo venture has grown into a dynamic consulting firm, supported by professionals with deep technical and functional expertise. Today, Succor Technical Services actively competes for government contracts and delivers services to a diverse portfolio of private sector clients.

Looking ahead, the company is focused on global expansion, bringing its expertise to organizations worldwide. In addition, Succor remains committed to community impact, with plans to support underprivileged students through future scholarship initiatives.

Our upcoming objective involves global expansion, extending our services to businesses worldwide. Additionally, Succor holds a strong commitment to supporting underprivileged communities. In the near future, we aspire to fund eligible students' school tuition through our scholarship program.
